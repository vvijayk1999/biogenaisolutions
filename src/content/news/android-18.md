Android 18 (人じん造ぞう人にん間げん１８号ごう *Jinzōningen Jū Hachi-Gō*, lit. "Artificial Human No. 18"), originally named Lazuli (ラズリ *Razuri*)[\[4]](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Android_18#cite_note-ASQ&A6-4) when she was an ordinary [human](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Earthling), is the older twin sister of [Android 17](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Android_17) and [Dr. Gero](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Dr._Gero)'s eighteenth [android](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Android) creation, designed to serve Gero's vendetta against [Goku](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Goku). While her interests do not initially deviate from this expectation, her curiosity to activate [Android 16](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Android_16), in spite of Gero's orders not to do so, leads Android 17 to take it upon himself to rebel and murder Gero. Eventually, Android 18 becomes a member of the [Z Fighters](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Dragon_Team), as well as the wife of [Krillin](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Krillin) and mother to their daughter [Marron](https://dragonball.fandom.com/wiki/Marron).
